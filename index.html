Music is everything

Sample is a diverse community, made up of developers, communicators, lawyers, researchers, and more. Together, we build the infrastructure, features, and experiences you love, and help shape the future of music.

Why

Because we believe in the value of ideas and information. Music is a great engine of innovation propelled by the imagination of its authors. We want to return control to the creative chain of the production of a work, optimizing and making the publication process transparent.

How

Automating the distribution of music and its royalties, so as not to depend on intermediaries and guarantee access to information. Allowing the artist to only worry about what really matters: his work.

What

A platform where the author can view income, compensation to third parties and royalty collection rights in real time. In addition, he can make the payment for his works automatically and directly.

We're changing the music industry

Sample is a music technology company founded on the premise that music services don't serve the needs of the artist. We want artists to take back what is theirs, the royalties and the power of their art. That is why our model is not based on traditional systems.

We believe in the future of the internet and blockchain technology to design a music publishing ecosystem, where artists do not need monopoly entities such as record companies.

This creates a more competitive market with better and lower cost options for common artists who are not well served by record labels and streaming services. We help drive innovation, inclusion and access across the industry.

Our Mission

Sample's mission is to unlock the potential of human creativity by giving creative artists the opportunity to make a living from their art and billions of fans the opportunity to enjoy and be inspired by it.

The race is on to find others who share our passion for building the future of music.
